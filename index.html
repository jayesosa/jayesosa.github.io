Hello, welcome to the REVERBERATOR.

Press the "Play" button to hear the result of Bach's Prelude in C minor with convolution reverb from various impulse responses of different spaces. Press the "stop" button to stop the file. Must refresh page to re-play.

Made with reverb.js. Reverb.js is dedicated to the public domain via the CC0 license.
